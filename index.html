Youpp by Ambrose Chua


Youpp
/yup/
noun
  1. A well-designed, simple YouTube client.

Youpp is a quick app to watch YouTube videos.

Designed for Mac OSX, Youpp is built to watch YouTube videos as a floating window, allowing you to watch videos while using other applications.

Youpp is an experimental application with no goals to achieve. But you'll have an awesome ad-free experience.

Technical specs

Running on node-webkit without the need for servers, Youpp can be easily modified without setting up an IDE.
